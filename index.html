Molecular Modeling and Structural Bioinformatics

5 Years

Additionally, I have experience in protein structure prediction (homology modeling), Alpha-Fold, and protein sequence analysis to understand protein-protein, protein-ligand, and protein-membrane systems and experienced on structural biology databases (PDB, UniProt, SCOP, Pfam).

Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD)

Currently applying CADD expertise as a Specialist at Aurigene Pharmaceutical Services, contributing to multiple drug discovery projects through structure- and ligand-based design of novel small molecule inhibitors. Also working on peptide and biomolecular design (proteins and enzymes) using structure-based approaches, reinforcement learning, and rule-based methods for therapeutic and industrial applications. Previously, I have utilized Structure-Based Drug Design techniques such as molecular docking, along with Ligand-Based Drug Design approaches including pharmacophore modeling, QSAR, drug similarity searches, and virtual screening, with a thorough understanding of ADMET, SAR, and Medicinal Chemistry principles.

ML/AI for Structural Bioinformatics and Drug Discovery

3 Years

I have gained experience with Deep Neural Networks (DNN), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN), Graph-based Neural Networks, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), and machine learning models like Logistic Regression, Linear Regression, Support Vector Machines (SVM), K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Random Forest, Naïve Bayes, XGBoost, and Decision Trees. I have applied these techniques to supervised and unsupervised learning tasks, including hotspot residue prediction in the 15LOX-1/PEBP1 protein complex, virtual screening, drug repurposing, and QSAR analysis.
